2.2-1 Express the function n³/1000 - 100n² - 100n + 3 in terms of Θ-notation.

n³/1000 - 100n² - 100n + 3
(1/1000) * n³ - 100 * n² - 100 * n + 3
a * n³ - b * n² - c * n + 3
an³ - bn² - cn + 3
n³ - n² - n + 3
Θ(n³)
